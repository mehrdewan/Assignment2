    PROJECT STRUCTURE

    At Sustainable Solutions, our project structure is designed to deliver comprehensive and sustainable solutions to our non-profit clients. Here's an overview of how our projects are structured:


  • Client Engagement: Each client is paired with a dedicated project team that consists of experienced professionals committed to our mission.

  • Project Duration: Projects are typically structured to last for a full semester, allowing ample time for in-depth analysis and strategy development.

  • Deliverables: Projects conclude with the presentation of findings and the delivery of completed, actionable deliverables as requested by the client. These deliverables are designed to empower the client to make informed decisions and drive positive change within their organization.
